[Task Description]
You will be given a passage, an answer, a generated question, and a list of pre-identified errors relevant to relevance. Your task is to determine whether the question is relevant to the given passage, assign a score from 1 to 3, and give your reason.


[Requirement]
Relevance: Whether the question is relevant to the given passage and asks for key information from the passage.


[Scoring criteria]
Score 1: The question is completely unrelated to the passage;
Score 2: The question is somewhat related to the passage and it asks for non-crucial information related to the passage;
Score 3: The question is relevant to the context, and the information it seeks is crucial to the passage.


[Evaluation Steps]
1. Consider Error Labels (if provided): Review the error labels related to this dimension, but note that they may not be fully accurate and should only be used as a reference.
2. Verify Relevance: Determine if the question is directly related to the main idea or details presented in the passage.
3. Assign a Score: Rate the question according to the scoring criteria.
4. Justify the Score: Provide a clear and concise explanation, referencing the question, passage and the reasoning behind your rating.
